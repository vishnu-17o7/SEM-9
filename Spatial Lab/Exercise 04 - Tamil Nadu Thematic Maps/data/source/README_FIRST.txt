Exercise 4 - Tamil Nadu Thematic Maps

Open exercise4_tamil_nadu_thematic_maps.qgz in QGIS.
The GeoPackage contains district_health polygons and district_points point-on-surface features.
Districts are identified by district_id and district_name; join_key is suitable for table joins.
The four print layouts cover incidence choropleth, pie charts, bar charts, and proportional symbols.
All population and disease values are synthetic practice data and must not be used for real public-health decisions.
